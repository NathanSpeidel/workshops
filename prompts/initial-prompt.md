
# Workshop Manager and Scheduler

## Overview

I would like your help making a website.

The purpose of this website would be to be used by employees of a company that leads educative workshops.

The application (web app) should be able to:

1. Provide an easy way for employees to enter data about upcoming workshops they will lead
2. Provide an easy way for exmployees to view their upcoming workshops (kind of like a calendar)
3. Provide an easy way for employees to enter data about the workshops they've completed
4. Provide an easy way for employees to view the data about the workshops they've lead


## Workshops Data

The data we collect about workshops falls into 2 categories:  The data we have before the workshop occurs, and the data we only get after the workhop is complete

### Initial Workshop Data (before the workshop)

* What is the service (can also be the "name" of the workshop)
* The school and location
* Date / time
* Classroom

### Post Workshop Data (after the workshop)

* Number of students
* Status (some times canceled, completed, etc)
